ADMISSIONS! ADMISSION!! ADMISSION!!!

At Great Success Model School(GSMS), we believe every child deserve access to quality education in a safe,engaging, and inclusive environment.Our admission process is designed to identify young learners with the potential to excel academically, socially and morally.

Why Choose Us?

  • A tradition of academic excellence and moral discipline.
  • Qualified and passionate educators.
  • Conducive learning environment with modern facilities.
  • Balanced curriculum focoused on intellectual, spiritual and physical growth.
  • Track record of outstanding performance in national examinations.

    • Admission Policy

    Admission into our school is open to boys and girls from diverse backgrounds who meet the school's academic and moral standard. We admit into:

  • Creche
  • Kindergarten(KG)
  • Nursery
  • Primary(Grade 1-6)

    • Admission Procedure

  • Purchase of Admission Form:
    • --Admission form can be obtained at the school premises or filled online through our website.
  • Entrance Examination:
    • --Candidate are assessed based on age-appropriate test in core subjects(English, Mathematics, General Knowledge
  • Oral Interview:
    • --Short Interview with the child and parents/guardians to understand the learner's background,communication, and behaviour
  • Review and Offer:
    • --Successful applicants will be contacted and offered admission with guildlines for enrollment.
  • Registration and Enrollment:
    • --Upon acceptance, parents/guardians complete registration by submitting required documents and fees.

    Admission Requirements.

  • Complete admission form
  • 2 recent passport-sixed photographs
  • Photocopy of birth Certificate or sworn declaration of age
  • Previous school's acdemic reports
